Best Time for Bird Watching

☀

Early Morning

6 AM – 9 AM

Birds are most active at sunrise, with soft golden light and calm trails.

☾

Evening

5 PM – 7 PM

Birds return to roost around dusk while the forest cools down.

●

Afternoon

1 PM – 4 PM

Low activity during hot hours, best for slow observation near shade.
